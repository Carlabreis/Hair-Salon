Treatments From The Gods

Here are some of the favourite treatments we offer. There are many options for every type of skin, hair, or just to help you unwind.

Galatea

Our milk facial treatment is loaded with essential nutrients that can benefit your skin in numerous ways. Vitamin D to accelerate the production of collagen, slows down ageing and wrinkles, diminishes dark spots and acne along with promoting skin elasticity. Vitamin B6, to promote formation of new cells, biotin that revitalizes the skin, proteins and vitamin B12 that help lighten skin tone and treat unevenness. It also contains lactic acid, potassium, calcium, magnesium and selenium.

Quicksilver

Hair mask used once or twice a week as an effective solution to restore shine, brightness, and manageability to stubborn grays. Gray hairs are finer and more fragile, the cuticle turns rough to the touch, and your strands become dehydrated and kinky. An ultra-hydrating formula that helps you regain proper moisture balance and infuse new life into your dull, brittle locks. A purple hue to neutralize the yellow brassiness brought on by environmental pollution and hair product dyes.

Pandora

Hydrated clay mask binds with not just toxins but also harmful bacteria and pathogens. When hydrated clay is applied to the skin and left to dry, as in the case of a clay mask, it binds to bacteria on the surface of the skin and deep in the pores. It brings the dried up bacteria to the surface of the skin to then be washed away. In other words, clay masks work as a natural antibiotic that can heal skin infections and even remedy issues like eczema, dermatitis, and psoriasis.
